Pelmeni

A Classic Russian Meal!

Pelmeni is a Russian dish, though not exlcusive to Russia or Russian people. Pelmeni are dumplings, and much like all cultures have their own version of it, so do the Russians. These dumplings are made with dough that includes mineral water as its water content and minced beef and pork, meats both common to Russia. What makes this dish uniquely Russian is the use of sour-cream as a topping

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Mineral Water
  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Pork
  • Sour-Cream
  • Seasonings of Choice

Steps:

  1. Make dough using Flour and Mineral Water
  2. Mix beef and pork, season
  3. Cut dough into circles, 1.5" wide
  4. Add 1 TBSP of meat-mix to each circle
  5. Fold each circle into dumpling shape
  6. Bring water in pot to boil
  7. Cook dumplings in water until they float
  8. Serve with Sour Cream!
