Lasagna

A Hearty Italian Meal!

Lasagna is a dish with an origin in Italian and

Italian-American culture. It is enjoyed, however

not merely by those of Italian ethnicity-- all enjoy it

for it is such a good dish. What makes it the great dish

that is is has to do with its heartiness.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna-cut pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Seasonings of Choice

Steps:

  1. Pre-Heat Oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Hear olive oil in skillet over medium-high hear
  3. Fry ground beef while seasoning
  4. Assemble in a baking pan sauce, noodles, and beef alternating each
  5. Bake in Oven for an Hour
