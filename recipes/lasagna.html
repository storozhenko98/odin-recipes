Lasagna

A Hearty Italian Meal!

Lasagna is a dish with an origin in Italian and

Italian-American culture. It is enjoyed, however

not merely by those of Italian ethnicity-- all enjoy it

for it is such a good dish. What makes it the great dish

that is is has to do with its heartiness.

Ingredients:

    Lasagna-cut pasta Tomato Sauce Ground Beef Mozzarella Cheese Parmesan Cheese Seasonings of Choice

Steps:

    Pre-Heat Oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit Hear olive oil in skillet over medium-high hear Fry ground beef while seasoning Assemble in a baking pan sauce, noodles, and beef alternating each Bake in Oven for an Hour
