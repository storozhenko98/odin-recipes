Garlic-Eggplant

A Classic Asian Meal!

Garlic-Eggplant is a classic Asian dish, often associated with the Chinese culture and people. It involved eggplants, garlic sauce, and often a protein of choice. This meal is hearty, and should be eaten with rice or bread.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplants
  • Garlic Sauce
  • Cooking Oil, High Heat
  • Protein of Choice

Steps:

  1. Cut Eggplants into Small / Medium Pieces
  2. Heat skillet on medium-high heat
  3. Add oil to skillet
  4. Fry eggplants in the pan until browned
  5. Lower skillet heat to medium
  6. Add protein of choice and cook through
  7. Add Garlic Sauce
  8. Serve with Rice!
